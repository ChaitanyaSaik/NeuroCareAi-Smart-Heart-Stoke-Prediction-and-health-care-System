Future Vision & Development Roadmap

NeuroCare AI is more than a prototype; it's a foundation for a comprehensive, preventive, and privacy-aware health ecosystem. Our roadmap includes:

Wearable Integration

Seamlessly connect with Fitbit, Apple HealthKit, and Mi Band for real-time vital monitoring and continuous insights.

IoT Anomaly Detection

Proactive identification of unusual health patterns from connected devices, enabling early intervention.

Federated Learning

Enhancing privacy by training models on decentralized user data, improving accuracy without compromising data ownership.

Multilingual Voice UI

Making healthcare accessible to diverse rural populations through intuitive, voice-activated interfaces.

Advanced XAI

Deeper Explainable AI with interactive SHAP visualizations for enhanced clinician and user trust.

Gamification & Community

Engaging users with health goals through rewards, challenges, and peer support networks.
